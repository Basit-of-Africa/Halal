# Halal Heart Throb

Pious connections for the modern Muslim. Built with Next.js, Tailwind CSS, ShadCN UI, and Genkit.

Halal Heart Throb is a comprehensive companion app designed to facilitate respectful connections for those seeking marriage and to strengthen the sacred bond between existing spouses. It combines lighthearted, faith-based humor with deep spiritual reflection and practical marital support.

## 🚀 Launching on Vercel

This project is optimized for Vercel deployment. Follow these steps to go live:

1.  **Push to GitHub**: Push your local code to a new GitHub repository.
2.  **Connect to Vercel**:
    - Log in to your [Vercel Dashboard](https://vercel.com).
    - Click **"Add New..."** and then **"Project"**.
    - Import your GitHub repository.
3.  **Configure Environment Variables**:
    - During the setup, click on the **Environment Variables** section.
    - Add `GOOGLE_GENAI_API_KEY` and set its value to your Gemini API key (from [Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com/)).
4.  **Deploy**: Click **"Deploy"**. Vercel will automatically detect the Next.js framework and build your app.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Framework**: Next.js 15 (App Router)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Components**: ShadCN UI (Radix UI)
- **AI**: Genkit with Google Gemini 1.5 Flash
- **Icons**: Lucide React
- **Storage**: Local Storage for user privacy

## 📖 Key Modules

### 💬 Pious Pickup Lines
Over 20+ respectful and clever Islamic-themed lines categorized by mood (Cheesy, Sincere, Spiritual, Funny). Includes one-tap copy and native sharing for those in the "halal search."

### 🤖 AI Halal Wingman
Generate custom pious lines using AI. Enter specific keywords (e.g., "coffee", "coding") and choose a mood, and our Genkit-powered wingman will craft a respectful, unique line tailored to your "intended's" interests.

### 💍 Marital Bond Game
Strengthen your relationship with 100 deep, Islamic-centered questions. Spouses can choose a session length (5-50 questions) to spark meaningful conversations and grow closer through shared values.

### 📍 Halal Date Ideas
Randomized date ideas inspired by the Sunnah and a halal lifestyle. From "Tahajjud & Tea" to "Sunnah Sports," keep the spark alive in a way that pleases Allah.

### ☀️ My Today With You (MTWY)
A personal appreciation log based on the Sunnah: *"He who does not thank people, does not thank Allah."* Log daily reasons why you appreciate your spouse and revisit them to cultivate a grateful heart.

### 🆘 SOS Support & Counselor Directory
A compassionate safety net for marriages facing difficulties. Provides spiritual encouragement from the Quran and a curated directory of faith-based counseling organizations.
- **Location-Aware**: Features a priority directory for Nigeria and global resources.
- **Smart Suggestions**: Users can detect their location to find the most relevant support nearby.

## ✨ Features

- **Privacy First**: All journal entries, favorites, and game progress are stored locally in your browser.
- **Islamic Boundaries**: AI-generated content is filtered to ensure it stays within respectful and pious limits.
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for mobile-first usage, perfect for quick access on the go.
- **One-Tap Actions**: Easily copy or share lines and date ideas with your partner.
